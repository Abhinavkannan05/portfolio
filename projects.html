Smart dustbin

The Smart Dustbin is a project aimed at revolutionizing waste management through automation. It utilizes an ultrasonic sensor, Arduino, and servo motor to enable a dustbin that opens its lid when a user approaches and closes after use. Additionally, an LED indicator alerts users when the dustbin is full. This system promotes hygienic, contactless waste disposal while improving operational efϐiciency. Implementation includes sensor calibration, motor control, and a full-bin detection mechanism. The project concludes that automated systems can greatly enhance cleanliness and convenience in both public and private spaces.

The Smart Dustbin is highly relevant to modern public and private environments. By addressing hygiene concerns and providing timely maintenance alerts, this system contributes to cleaner surroundings and promotes the adoption of smart technologies for urban waste management.
